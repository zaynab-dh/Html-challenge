Mandala

What is mandala?

Mandala is a Sanskrit word that loosely translates to mean circle or center.

Mandalas are found across all cultures

  • Celtic spirals and knot work
  • Christianity – rose windows, rosary, halos
  • Chinese Yin and Yang symbol
  • Hindu Yantras
  • and others
Benefits of Creating Mandalas Relaxes the body and mind Cultivates the feeling of happiness, inner peace, and general well-being Eases stress, anxiety, worry, overwhelm, fear, and depression Activates creativity and improves focus Enhances self-esteem and self-acceptance It’s fun!

Once you discover what a mandala is you’ll begin to see them everywhere!
